Who am I?

Before Re:Coded.

My name is Ahmed Salim, and I was born in Iraq-Anbar. I graduated from Agriculture College in 2009. I have a variety of work experience, and always wanted to learn English and skills in website design. I love to read history, watch movies and sports.I am curious to learn new things.

Why I joined Re:Coded

This course represented a very important opportunity to improve my qualifications and improve my life, by getting a good job in this emerging field which is quite a new field in Iraq and the Middle East. It gives me the advantage of getting a good job when I finish my studies.

What After Re:Coded ?

I will continue my studies and review everything I have learned to improve my skills and learn new skills. I also hope to get a good job which suits my ambitions and uses all the skills and information that I learned. I will also keep the most important thing I received during the course, which are my friendships. I will never forget that we started as colleagues and then became friends and finished our studies as a family.
